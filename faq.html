Jewellery Care

For gold jewellery, gently clean with a soft cloth and mild dish soap diluted in warm water. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry. Avoid exposing jewellery to chemicals, perfumes, chlorine, and saltwater. For diamond pieces, use a soft toothbrush with soapy water. We recommend professional cleaning once a year at our atelier (free for LUMIÈRE customers).

Store each piece separately in the LUMIÈRE velvet pouch or box provided to prevent scratching. Keep jewellery away from direct sunlight and humidity. Silver pieces should be stored in anti-tarnish pouches. Avoid piling jewellery together in a drawer, as this can cause scratches and damage delicate settings.
