Our Story

Crafting Timeless
Elegance Since 2005

LUMIÈRE Jewels was born from a single conviction: that every piece of jewellery should tell a story. Founded in the heart of Hyderabad's historic jewellery district, we began as a small atelier with three master craftsmen and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Two decades later, we have grown into one of India's most revered luxury jewellery houses — yet our philosophy remains unchanged. Every ring, necklace, and bracelet that leaves our workshop is hand-inspected, certified, and crafted with the same passion that ignited our very first creation.

How We Work

The Making of a Masterpiece

01
Design & Ideation

Our design team sketches each concept by hand, drawing inspiration from Mughal architecture, nature, and contemporary global trends. Every design undergoes 12 refinement cycles.

02
Stone Selection

Each gemstone is individually evaluated for the 4Cs — cut, colour, clarity, and carat. Only stones meeting our strict criteria earn a place in a LUMIÈRE creation.

03
Master Craftsmanship

Skilled artisans spend between 40 to 200 hours on each piece, employing hand-engraving, pave-setting, and filigree techniques passed down through generations.

04
Quality Inspection

Every finished piece undergoes a rigorous 14-point quality inspection by our senior gemologist before receiving the LUMIÈRE seal of approval.

05
BIS Certification

All gold jewellery carries an official BIS hallmark. Diamond pieces are accompanied by a GIA or IGI certificate, guaranteeing authenticity.

06
Luxury Packaging

Your creation is nestled in a handcrafted velvet box, wrapped in silk tissue, and presented in our signature LUMIÈRE gift box — ready to create a lasting impression.
